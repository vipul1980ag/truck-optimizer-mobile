Privacy Policy

Truck Load Optimizer  |  Effective date: May 1, 2026  |  Last updated: May 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how DNW-AI / Vipul Agrawal ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Truck Load Optimizer mobile application and web platform (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you register, we collect your email address, phone number, and delivery address.

Cargo and shipment data: Information you enter about items, routes, customers, and bookings to operate the Service.

Photos: Images you upload or take within the app are sent to our AI service for item identification. Photos are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers.

Location data: If you search for pickup or delivery locations, the text you enter is sent to a geocoding service (OpenStreetMap Nominatim / Komoot Photon) to return address suggestions. We do not collect or store your device GPS location.

Payment information: Payments are processed by PayPal. We do not store credit card or bank account details.

Usage data: Standard server logs (IP address, request timestamps) are retained for up to 30 days for security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Service
  • To authenticate your account and maintain session security
  • To process bookings and calculate shipping costs
  • To communicate important service updates

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini / Anthropic Claude — AI-powered photo item recognition. Images are transmitted securely and subject to their respective privacy policies.
  • OpenStreetMap / Komoot Photon / Nominatim — Location search and routing.
  • PayPal — Payment processing.
  • Railway.app — Cloud hosting provider.

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your account and shipment data is stored on secure servers hosted by Railway.app. We use HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and are never stored in plain text.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and shipment data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for business use and is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
